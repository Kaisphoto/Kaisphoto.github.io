About Me

Raised in Shanghai, shaped in LA, Liverpool and Xi'an. Now resident in Stockholm, Sweden, where he pursues a degree in Biostatistics at the Karolinska Institutet while also working as a photographer.
His photography is an extension of his personal journey, a vibrant tapestry that captures the essence of real life, meticulously exploring the subtle nuances of everyday moments and those extraordinary instants. Passionate about exploring sports culture and beyond, he focuses on capturing sensory experiences that connect people, sports, and nature. No matter where he is—whether immersed in academic pursuits, enjoying time with family, or sharing moments with friends—he always finds a sense of belonging.
